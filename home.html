DISCOVER THE FEST

The Noorul Huda Literature Festival is a committed and authentic endeavour to express and discuss Costal Karnataka's rich history and cultural heritage. Both academic and popular in nature, this focused festival brings about an array of topics that are urgent and significant for the people of this place. To be held in Madannoor from December 7,8, 2023.NLF creates a unique space where distinguished laureates, thinkers, social activists and enthusiasts come together to explore the cultural diversity of Karnataka and to unfold the literary elegance and tranquil coexistence of Tulu, Beary. The festival's themes honor Karnataka's rich heritage, weaving together history, language, and art to foster a deeper connection among communities.An initiative of NSU, the Student Association of Noorul Huda Islamic Academy, located in the serene village of Madannoor. We bring NLF to the community, promoting creativity, intellectual growth, and cultural pride.
